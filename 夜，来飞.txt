然后和喧嚣一起
被风吞没无踪迹

可是听不清回音

请带走我
和我所有的彷徨

还要倒数
多少说不清的悲凉

是我的一生彷徨

曾经那泪水模糊枕巾的夜晚

卷走了最后一点信仰

听过雨点滴落在每一个季节

任凭情绪低落在每一刻思念

囚禁在外面
拖着残缺的躯壳
暴风雨打在身上

The late night wind
Disturbing the peace of summer
The lost person
Took away the finished letters

Someone sings in the wind
Then along with the noise
Swallowed up by the wind without a trace
Someone running in the rain
Like shouting your name
But I can't hear the echo

The late night wind
Please take me away
And all my wanderings
The sleepless night
And count down
How many indescribable sadnesses
The slightest hesitation of yours
Is my lifetime of uncertainty
Your moment of hesitation
Is my whole life of sorrow

Your one-inch photo
My only one
Your exclusive memory
I will treasure the pain

The thunder is white half of the sky
Once that thunderous stubbornness
Now I dare to put down my dreams lightly
The rainstorm wetted half of the curtain
The night that tears blurred the pillowcase
Now I can't learn to be strong
But forget to fantasize
Until the wind
Swept away the last bit of faith

I've been in this world
I've heard the raindrops fall in every season
I have come to this time
Letting my emotions fall in every moment of thought

Close the window
Let this cruel and cold world
Imprisoned outside
Dragging a broken body
The storms hitting my body
Gradually
No more pain
